About IEEE
MBCCET SB

IEEE is the world’s largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through IEEE’s highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. The prime motive behind IEEE Student Branch at MBCCET is to make the students be a part of the largest technological community in the world. It’s a global platform to expose their technical ideas as well as to make connections with great personalities.

Why IEEE?
